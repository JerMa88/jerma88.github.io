## 
There are so many thoughts, 

And so many things to learn.

So many things to improve,

So many ways to turn. 

I want to succeed, 

But I must fail first.

I may not be the best,

But I'm far from the worst.


## 
The ambiguity of idioms, what does it mean?

Drifting in the wind, like a leaf in a stream.

Carrying a hope in the land in between,

The land of the living, the land of the dream.

The future is bright, but the past is unseen,

The present is now, but the past is serene.

The past is a memory, the future is a dream,

The present is now, but the past is a scream.

We comfortably assume, that this is the key, 

To automating language with a machine.

But the diversity of language isn't obsolete,

How can we capture it all with just MSE?


## 
Alone and lonely, lonely and alone,

Hiding and seeking behind a big stone. 

Chasing a tick, circling a zone, 

Fabricating a truth not so well sown. 

Causing a scene, a name unbeknown, 

The time of fame it simply outgrown.

Resting his case, as a fly moans,

T'was really just me, lonely and alone.


## 
Stand at the feet of a unclimable mountain,

What constitutes human excellence, or emotion?

The world is a stage, and we are the actors,

And we must overcome adversity, be its master.

This climb is steep, and the air is thin,

But we are not here to fail, but to win.

There are setbacks, and but we must persist,

Stand back up and fight, and never give in.


## 
I love that you are always in my pain. 

Even when I think you're gone, you remain.

I relive you when I hear those songs,

Why not, you are fed by people's wants.

I feel that panic, when I sense you there, 

Because what you say is true and dear.

I am aware that life is not very fair, 

But the way you are still here, 

Keeps me wondering if I am going nowhere.


## 
Sure I escaped those lies, but I can't escape the fate, that I will die as I cry. 

The free world offers me light, but I rather stay in that delusional chime. 

For wisdom brings me sorrow, and I rather have not seen hopes of joyous time,

Now that I have none, and all that I do, is to grind for that merciless, worthless dime.


## 
As I stand here like Ozymandias, 

I rest my weight on this air, Alexandra.

No amount of promises can ease my paranoia, 

As I defeat my own longing in my metanoia.
