I am Zerui “Jerry” Ma, an undergraduate researcher at Southern Methodist University in Dallas, Texas. I major in B.S. in Computer Science with a specialization in AI/ML, B.S. in Data Science and B.A. in Mathematics.

I am a Research Assistant working with Dr. Mehak Gupta for her current research in deep learning and NLP with applications in healthcare, where my responsibility has been fine-tuning LLM with interview transcripts for personality and psychological disorder detection (Click here for research abstract).

I have an independent research topic on a Course Recommender System for University Curriculum (Click here for research abstract) mentored by Dr. Michael Hahsler of Computer Science at the Bobby B. Lyle School of Engineering, and Dr. Peter Moore of Mathematics at the Dedman College of Humanities and Sciences. The research has been published to AAAI Spring Symposium and to NCUR. It will be published for the Robert Mayer Fellowship by the end of the 2024-2025 school year.

I am looking for a Ph.D. position in Computer Science, with a focus on Machine Learning Theory and Natural Language Processing.
